Vrijwilligersparaat

Temporary site to coordinate volunteer efforts during the COVID-19 crisis in Belgium.


A simple flask app with jinja2 templates and an SQLite database. Made voluntarily on behalf of the Flemish Support Center for Volunteer Work / "Vlaams Steunpunt Vrijwilligerswerk".
